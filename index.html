Statistics

Studies show that over 30% of traffic accidents in Saudi Arabia are caused by speeding. Statistics reveal that accidents result in over 6,000 fatalities annually, along with severe injuries to thousands of individuals.

Main Causes of Accidents

  • Speeding.
  • Using mobile phones while driving.
  • Disregarding traffic rules and signals.
  • Lack of regular vehicle maintenance.

Efforts to Reduce Accidents

Saudi Arabia has launched various initiatives to enhance road safety, such as increasing the number of traffic cameras and implementing advanced systems like "Saher." Intensive awareness campaigns are also conducted to educate citizens about the importance of safe driving.
